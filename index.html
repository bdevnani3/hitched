How do we do what we do?

All we do is find you the most optimal carpool buddy to minimize the length of your trip. Using the Esri API we were able to not only consider people with the same start and end points, but also people who want to be picked up and dropped off enroute. We keep a threshold to make sure that the rerouting is feasible and the delay is absolutely minimal. The recommender system is built keeping in mind these constraints.

Why carpooling?

I noticed that there weren't a lot of apps that allowed you to carpool to and from common destinations. Whether it was going from the airport to my school or even across a couple of states to a hackathon, there was no common platform that adressed this issue.

Why is hitched different?

While thinking of hitched, I didn't want it to be a taxi service from point A to B, but make use of the fact that this wasn't a completely professional service. There are often cases when a person can be dropped enroute or just at a couple minutes detour. The algorithm used for this app makes use of that. The general thresh hold is the detour shouldn't cause a delay more than 8% of the total trip time of the actual driver. Thus keeping these constraints in mind, and using ESRI's awesome API, this carpooling app recommends ideal drivers to people requesting a trip and then gives the drivers a chance to accept or reject a request.
